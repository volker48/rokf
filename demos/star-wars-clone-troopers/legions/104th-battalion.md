The **104th Battalion**, nicknamed the **Wolfpack**, was a clone
trooper battalion led by Jedi General Plo Koon. They specialized in
rescue missions and rapid-response deployments, often operating in
high-risk deep-space environments. Their armor bore grey wolf-themed
markings.

# Command Structure

| Position       | Officer       |
|----------------|---------------|
| Jedi General   | Plo Koon      |
| Clone Commander | Commander Wolffe |

# Equipment

The 104th deployed with [Phase II Armor](/equipment/phase-ii-armor.md)
featuring grey wolf markings, and carried [DC-15A Blaster Rifles](/equipment/dc-15a-blaster-rifle.md).
