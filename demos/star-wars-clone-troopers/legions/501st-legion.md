The **501st Legion** was a brigade-sized elite military unit of clone
troopers in the Grand Army of the Republic. Known for aggressive and
unconventional tactics, they were among the most decorated units in the
Republic military. Their distinctive blue markings set them apart on
any battlefield.

# Command Structure

| Position       | Officer                |
|----------------|------------------------|
| Jedi General   | Anakin Skywalker       |
| Clone Captain  | [Captain Rex](/personnel/captain-rex.md) |

# Notable Members

* [Captain Rex](/personnel/captain-rex.md) — Captain of Torrent Company and trusted second-in-command.
* [Fives](/personnel/fives.md) — ARC Trooper who rose through the ranks of Torrent Company.

# Key Engagements

* [Battle of Umbara](/battles/battle-of-umbara.md) — A grueling campaign against Umbaran separatist forces.
* [Siege of Mandalore](/battles/siege-of-mandalore.md) — The final confrontation of the Clone Wars.

# Equipment

The 501st fielded standard [DC-15A Blaster Rifles](/equipment/dc-15a-blaster-rifle.md)
and wore [Phase II Armor](/equipment/phase-ii-armor.md) with blue
legion markings. Officers carried [DC-17 Hand Blasters](/equipment/dc-17-hand-blaster.md).
