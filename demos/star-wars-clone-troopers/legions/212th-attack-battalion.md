The **212th Attack Battalion** served as the main offensive unit of
the 7th Sky Corps under High General Obi-Wan Kenobi. Known for their
discipline and effectiveness in combined-arms operations, the 212th
saw action across the galaxy. Their armor bore distinctive orange
markings.

# Command Structure

| Position           | Officer                    |
|--------------------|----------------------------|
| Jedi High General  | Obi-Wan Kenobi             |
| Marshal Commander  | [Commander Cody](/personnel/commander-cody.md) |

# Notable Members

* [Commander Cody](/personnel/commander-cody.md) — Marshal Commander and Obi-Wan's trusted right hand.

# Equipment

The 212th relied on [DC-15A Blaster Rifles](/equipment/dc-15a-blaster-rifle.md)
and [Phase II Armor](/equipment/phase-ii-armor.md) with orange markings.
