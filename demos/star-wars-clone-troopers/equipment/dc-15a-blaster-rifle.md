The **DC-15A Blaster Rifle** was the primary long-range infantry weapon
of the Grand Army of the Republic. Manufactured by BlasTech Industries,
it fired blue plasma bolts and was known for accuracy at long range.
Though heavier than the carbine variant, its stopping power made it the
standard-issue rifle for frontline clone troopers.
