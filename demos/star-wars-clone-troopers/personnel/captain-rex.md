**CT-7567**, known as **Captain Rex**, was a veteran clone captain who
served under Jedi General Anakin Skywalker and commanded Torrent
Company within the [501st Legion](/legions/501st-legion.md). Loyal,
tactically brilliant, and fiercely protective of his men, Rex was one
of the most respected officers in the Grand Army of the Republic.

# Unit Affiliation

Rex served in the [501st Legion](/legions/501st-legion.md) for the
duration of the Clone Wars.

# Key Traits

* **Tactical acumen** — Known for creative battlefield solutions.
* **Loyalty** — Unwavering dedication to his brothers and his general.
* **Independence** — Willing to question orders when facts warranted it.

# Equipment

Rex wielded dual [DC-17 Hand Blasters](/equipment/dc-17-hand-blaster.md)
as his signature sidearms, wore custom [Phase II Armor](/equipment/phase-ii-armor.md)
with blue 501st markings and a distinctive jaig-eyed helmet, and carried
a [DC-15A Blaster Rifle](/equipment/dc-15a-blaster-rifle.md) for
long-range engagements.

# Key Engagements

* [Battle of Umbara](/battles/battle-of-umbara.md) — Led the 501st through one of its darkest campaigns.
* [Siege of Mandalore](/battles/siege-of-mandalore.md) — Commanded the 501st in the final battle of the Clone Wars.
