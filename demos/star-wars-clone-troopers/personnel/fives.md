**CT-5555**, nicknamed **Fives**, was an ARC Trooper in the
[501st Legion](/legions/501st-legion.md)'s Torrent Company. He rose
from a rookie to one of the most decorated troopers in the legion
before uncovering a conspiracy that threatened every clone in the
Grand Army.

# Unit Affiliation

Fives served in the [501st Legion](/legions/501st-legion.md) under
[Captain Rex](/personnel/captain-rex.md).

# Key Traits

* **Courage** — Volunteered for dangerous assignments without hesitation.
* **Curiosity** — Followed evidence that others dismissed.
* **Sacrifice** — Gave his life trying to warn the Republic about the inhibitor chips.

# The Inhibitor Chip Discovery

Fives discovered that every clone carried a bio-chip implanted at
creation that could override their free will — knowledge that would
prove critical to understanding Order 66.

# Equipment

As an ARC Trooper, Fives wore modified [Phase II Armor](/equipment/phase-ii-armor.md)
with ARC-specific kama and pauldron attachments and carried dual
[DC-17 Hand Blasters](/equipment/dc-17-hand-blaster.md).
