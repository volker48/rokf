**CC-2224**, known as **Commander Cody**, served as Marshal Commander
of the 7th Sky Corps under High General Obi-Wan Kenobi. Professional,
disciplined, and fiercely capable, Cody was one of the highest-ranking
clone officers in the Grand Army of the Republic and the face of the
[212th Attack Battalion](/legions/212th-attack-battalion.md).

# Unit Affiliation

Cody commanded the [212th Attack Battalion](/legions/212th-attack-battalion.md)
throughout the Clone Wars and served directly under General Kenobi on
countless campaigns.

# Key Traits

* **Discipline** — Meticulous planner who insisted on thorough preparation.
* **Loyalty** — Deeply committed to his men and his general.
* **Pragmatism** — Valued practical outcomes over glory.

# Equipment

Cody wore [Phase II Armor](/equipment/phase-ii-armor.md) with orange 212th
markings and used a [DC-15A Blaster Rifle](/equipment/dc-15a-blaster-rifle.md)
as his primary weapon.
