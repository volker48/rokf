The **Siege of Mandalore** was the climactic final battle of the Clone
Wars. The [501st Legion](/legions/501st-legion.md) — led by
[Captain Rex](/personnel/captain-rex.md) and the former Jedi Ahsoka
Tano — assaulted the Mandalorian capital of Sundari to capture the
former Sith Lord Maul. The siege concluded with Maul's capture, but the
victory turned to tragedy when Order 66 was executed shortly afterward.

# Notable Participants

* [Captain Rex](/personnel/captain-rex.md) — Commanded clone forces.

# Equipment Used

The 332nd Company wore [Phase II Armor](/equipment/phase-ii-armor.md)
with distinctive orange helmets honoring Ahsoka Tano and carried
[DC-15A Blaster Rifles](/equipment/dc-15a-blaster-rifle.md).

# Outcome

Maul was captured, but during the return journey Order 66 was
executed. Rex resisted the order long enough to help Ahsoka escape
before the Republic became the Empire.
