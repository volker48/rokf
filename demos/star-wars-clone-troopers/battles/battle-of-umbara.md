The **Battle of Umbara** was one of the grimmest campaigns of the Clone
Wars. The [501st Legion](/legions/501st-legion.md) and
[212th Attack Battalion](/legions/212th-attack-battalion.md) were
deployed to the shadowy world of Umbara to seize the capital from
separatist-aligned Umbaran forces. The engagement turned tragic when
Jedi General Pong Krell deliberately misled clone forces, causing
fratricide and mass casualties.

# Forces Involved

| Unit                        | Commander               |
|-----------------------------|-------------------------|
| [501st Legion](/legions/501st-legion.md)              | Anakin Skywalker (recalled), then Pong Krell  |
| [212th Attack Battalion](/legions/212th-attack-battalion.md) | [Commander Cody](/personnel/commander-cody.md) |

# Notable Participants

* [Captain Rex](/personnel/captain-rex.md) — Led the 501st after Skywalker was recalled.
* [Commander Cody](/personnel/commander-cody.md) — Commanded the 212th alongside Krell.
* [Fives](/personnel/fives.md) — Fought on the front lines.

# Equipment Used

Troopers deployed with [Phase II Armor](/equipment/phase-ii-armor.md)
and [DC-15A Blaster Rifles](/equipment/dc-15a-blaster-rifle.md).

# Outcome

The 501st and 212th ultimately captured the Umbaran capital after
confronting and arresting the traitorous General Krell.
